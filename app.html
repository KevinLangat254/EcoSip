The EcoSip Companion App

Track Your Hydration

The EcoSip app syncs via Bluetooth to give you real-time data on your water intake.

Key App Features:

  • Daily Goals: Set custom hydration targets based on your weight and activity.
  • History: View weekly and monthly graphs of your progress.
  • Find My Bottle: Locate your bottle if you leave it behind.
  • Glow Settings: Customize the color of the LED reminder light.

Available on iOS and Android.
