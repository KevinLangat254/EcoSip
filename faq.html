Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the EcoSip bottle dishwasher safe?

A: The stainless steel body is dishwasher safe. However, the smart lid (electronics) must be hand-washed.


Q: How do I charge the bottle?

A: It comes with a magnetic USB-C charger. A full charge takes about 2 hours.


Q: Do I need the app to use the bottle?

A: No, the bottle will still glow to remind you to drink, but you need the app to see your history and change settings.


Q: What is the warranty?

A: We offer a 1-year warranty on the electronic lid and a lifetime warranty on the steel bottle body.
